The story of the rivalry between The University of Georgia Bulldogs, and the Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets.

This historic rivalry between the two Georgia schools mainly revoves around the sport of American football.The schools are vastly differnt, with the University of Georgia being mainly an agricultural and bussiness school, located in rual Athens, Georgia. While Georgia Tech is known for being an engineering school, and is located in the big city of Atlanta. Both teams have won national titles in football. Georgia won in 1942 and 1980, While Georgia Tech won in 1917, 1928, 1952 and 1990. The first meeting of these teams was on November 4th, 1893. Both teams have their unique school traditions and mascots. The University of Georgia dawns the colors red and black, and have a Bulldog as their mascot. While Georgia Tech dawns the inferior colors gold and white, and have a whimpy Yellow Jacket as their mascot. Hate for the opposing team is strongly ingrained in the traditions and fight songs of each team. Georgia students commonly refer to Tech students as nerds, dorks, or Techies, while tech refers to Georgia students as rednecks, and mocks them in almost all of their fight songs. Georgia dosent mock Teck in their fight songs because, UGA is a classier university and sees Tech as being less important.

The campuses, stadiums, and student population of each school are quite differnt as well. The University of Georgia has a larger campus, with the main campus being 762 acres compared to Georgia Tech's campus size of 372 acres. Georgia has a much larger student body of 36,230 students to Georgia Tech's 29,370. UGA's Stadium, Sanford Stadium, is the 10th largest stadium in the NCAA and has the capacity to hold 92,746 fans. This is much larger then Georgia Tech's Bobby Dobb Stadium, which holds up to 55,000 fans.
